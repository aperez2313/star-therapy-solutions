About the Owner

Victoria Perez, M.S. CCC-SLP is a bilingual Speech-Language Pathologist and the founder of Star Therapy Solutions. She is licensed to practice in the state of Florida and has extensive experience providing pediatric speech and language services in school and daycare settings.

Victoria specializes in the evaluation and treatment of speech and language delays, articulation disorders, social communication challenges, fluency, and voice disorders. Her clinical approach emphasizes individualized care, functional goals, and collaboration with families and educators to support meaningful progress for every child.

Meet Our Team

Star Therapy Solutions is supported by a team of licensed, bilingual speech-language pathologists and occupational therapists experienced in providing pediatric therapy services in school and daycare settings.

Our clinicians deliver one-on-one, individualized therapy and collaborate closely with families and educators to support functional progress across daily routines and learning environments.

All team members practice in accordance with Florida Department of Health standards and professional scope of practice.

Our Clinical Approach

Star Therapy Solutions uses evidence-based practices guided by ongoing data collection, clinical expertise, and family collaboration. Therapy goals are functional, measurable, and individualized to support meaningful participation across school routines, home activities, and social environments.

Intervention strategies are continuously adjusted based on each child's response to treatment to ensure progress remains appropriate, measurable, and developmentally relevant.
