Frequently Asked Questions

Everything families ask before getting started.

Do you provide therapy at my child's school or daycare?

Yes. Star Therapy Solutions provides one-on-one pediatric speech and occupational therapy services on-site at private schools, charter schools, public schools, and licensed daycares within our service areas.

Please note that Star Therapy Solutions is a private pediatric therapy practice. While services are delivered on-site at schools and daycares, therapy is not provided through the school district and does not replace county or public school services.

How do I know if my child needs speech or occupational therapy?

Children may benefit from therapy if they demonstrate delays in communication, fine motor skills, sensory processing, attention, or daily living skills. A free developmental screening can help determine whether a comprehensive evaluation is recommended.

When are therapy sessions scheduled?

Therapy sessions are typically scheduled during the school day and coordinated with the school and family to minimize disruption to classroom instruction.

What ages do you work with?

Star Therapy Solutions provides pediatric speech and occupational therapy services to children attending preschools, daycares, and school settings. Services are individualized based on developmental needs rather than age alone.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes. Star Therapy Solutions accepts select Medicaid plans, select commercial insurance plans, private pay, and the Step Up for Students – Unique Abilities Grant, when applicable.

How do I get started?

Families may contact Star Therapy Solutions directly to schedule a free screening or to learn more about available services. Our team will confirm service availability based on school or daycare location.

Still have questions?

We’re happy to answer them. Reach out and we’ll respond within 1 business day.
