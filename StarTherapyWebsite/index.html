About Star Therapy Solutions

Star Therapy Solutions is a therapist-owned private practice providing pediatric speech therapy and occupational therapy services on-site at your child's school or daycare.

We partner with families, private schools, charter schools, public schools, and licensed daycares to deliver individualized, evidence-based therapy in familiar learning environments where children are most comfortable and engaged.

Star Therapy Solutions is a private practice and is not affiliated with or employed by the school district. Services are delivered through family referral and school collaboration.

Our Services

Therapy services are provided directly at your child's school or daycare — allowing for natural carryover of skills into daily routines, classroom activities, and peer interactions.

  • Pediatric Speech Therapy
  • Occupational Therapy
  • On-Site School & Daycare Services
  • No clinic visits. No extra travel.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers.

What areas does Star Therapy Solutions serve?

We serve schools and daycares in Miami Lakes, Hialeah, Palm Springs North, Hialeah Gardens, and surrounding Miami-Dade communities. Service availability depends on school location and current caseload — contact us to check your area.

Does Star Therapy Solutions accept Medicaid or insurance?

Yes. We accept select Medicaid plans, select commercial insurance plans, private pay, and the Step Up for Students Unique Abilities Grant when applicable. Contact us to verify your specific plan before initiating services.

What therapy services do you offer?

We offer pediatric speech therapy and pediatric occupational therapy. All sessions are provided on-site at your child's school or daycare — no clinic visits or extra travel required.

Where do therapy sessions take place?

Sessions happen on-site at your child's school or daycare. This allows for natural carryover of skills into daily classroom routines, activities, and peer interactions — making therapy more effective.

How do I get started with therapy for my child?

Fill out the contact form below or call us at (305) 778-6144. We'll verify availability at your child's school or daycare and reach out within 1 business day to walk you through next steps.

Is Star Therapy Solutions affiliated with the school district?

No. Star Therapy Solutions is a private practice and is not affiliated with or employed by the school district. Services are arranged through family referral and collaboration with the school or daycare.
